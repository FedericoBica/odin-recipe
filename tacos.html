Mexican Tacos

The tacos are a delicious dish you should try

Ingredients

  • 6 corn tortillas
  • 350g minced beef or chicken
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 500g tomatoes
  • salt and pepper
  • oil
  • grated cheese

To make the pico de gallo

  • 1 small onion chopped
  • 1 jalape√±o
  • medium tomatoes
  • A few coriander leaves
  • 1/2 lime or 1/2 lemon

Steps

  1. To start with our Mexican tacos, we chop the garlic and cook it over a very low heat for 1 minute. that does not take color
  2. Add half a kilo of tomatoes, already peeled and chopped, and increase the power of the fire. Season and cook for a while. The tomato will release water, all that should evaporate, it may take a while. Once evaporated, let it cook over low heat while stirring constantly, for 5 more minutes, so that it takes on a good flavor
  3. While the water from the tomatoes evaporates, prepare the pico de gallo. Pico de gallo is a sauce that suits Mexican tacos. In a bowl we put all the vegetables of this well chopped sauce. Add the juice of 1/2 lime or half a lemon and stir well. Let rest
  4. When the tomato has cooked a bit, we add the minced meat that we have chosen. Add your share of salt and pepper and cook over high heat. With a wooden spoon, we are removing everything and shredding the minced meat, so that there are no large pieces. In fact, the finer they are, the better. So we mix with the tomato and garlic, while we shred it and cook the meat
  5. Once it's cooked, it's ready! Heat the tortillas one by one on both sides in a skillet over medium heat. Then we put a few tablespoons of the meat on top, season with a little pico de gallo, wrap them giving it the characteristic taco shape. And it's ready! Optionally you can also sprinkle grated cheese on top.
